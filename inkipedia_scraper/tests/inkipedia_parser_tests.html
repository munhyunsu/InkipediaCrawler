22 April 2022
Splatoon 3 key artwork

You heard it, squidkids - Splatoon 3 is officially releasing on Nintendo Switch on 9 September 2022! That means you have over four months to practice your splatting... or get your body ready to join in for the first time! There's loads of new information hiding in the release date trailer, and we can't wait to meet you in the Splatlands!

Oh, and while we're here, the Octo Expansion is now available for free for anybody with a Nintendo Switch Online + Expansion Pack subscription!

10 April 2022

Inkipedia is available in another language - Spanish! This new Spanish-language wiki is called Inkipedia ES or Inkipedia en espaol.

The project started as a FANDOM wiki on 20 March 2015, and became an independent wiki with the creation of Inkipedia ES on 5 March 2022. Inkipedia ES was opened to the public for editing on 8 April 2022.

This is the second time we have launched another version of Inkipedia to support a language; the first language to be added was French with the opening of Inkipedia FR on 8 June 2016.

Inkipedia ES already has over 400 articles for you to read and edit. Anyone can create an Inkipedia ES account to join the community!

Little buddy has gone missing! Agent 3 has been looking everywhere, but they need your help!

Little buddy's two favorite spoons are missing as well. It's up to you to find them! There is a little buddy or spoon hidden in 3 Inkipedia articles.

Top prize: Find little buddy and both spoons, and win a little buddy plush!
Basic prize: Find at least one spoon or little buddy, and win access to the limited-access Splatoon 3 Testfire next week!

Splatoon 3 Testfire details: You can play online battles with four weapons, in the Scorch Gorge and Eeltail Alley stages. The weapons are Splattershot, Splat Roller, Splat Charger and a new weapon with a shape like a bow!
